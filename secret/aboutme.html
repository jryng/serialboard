You're already here anyway, so I guess I'll tell you about this project. Strangely, work on it started back in early November - I made some generic assets in case I got a Serial-related project, namely the vectorized logo and overall color pallet (if you haven't noticed, the paper on the corkboard has the same colour scheme as the Serial website, a type of cream yellow.) It was just that predictable.

I've recently been trying out some new techniques for making my designs, mainly related to 3D. I use 3DS Max for my modeling purposes, Photoshop and Pixplant for texturing, Keyshot 7 for rendering and Coreldraw for everything in between. Seriously - it's one of the most useful pieces of software I've ever used, even if it's a bit antiquated and buggy at times. I use it for compositing, vector art, even building web pages (see index)!

That's about it for info about this project. You've found one of two secrets on the main page. Try to find the rest!
